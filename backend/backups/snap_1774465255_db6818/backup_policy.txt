BACKUP & RECOVERY POLICY
========================
Version: 3.2 | Approved: 2025-11-01
Owner: IT Operations

BACKUP SCHEDULE
---------------
Full Backup:        Every Sunday 02:00 AM
Incremental:        Daily Mon-Sat 02:00 AM
Snapshot (SentinelShield): Every 30 minutes (automated)

RETENTION POLICY
----------------
Daily backups:      30 days
Weekly backups:     12 weeks
Monthly backups:    12 months
Snapshots:          72 hours rolling

RECOVERY OBJECTIVES
-------------------
RTO (Recovery Time Objective):   4 hours
RPO (Recovery Point Objective):  30 minutes

STORAGE LOCATIONS
-----------------
Primary:    /app/backups (local NVMe — immutable)
Secondary:  Azure Blob Storage (geo-redundant)
Offsite:    Physical tape — monthly rotation

RANSOMWARE RESPONSE PROCEDURE
------------------------------
1. SentinelShield detects canary file access
2. Automatic snapshot triggered immediately
3. Infected files quarantined to /app/quarantine
4. Restore from last clean snapshot
5. Incident report filed within 2 hours
6. Post-mortem within 48 hours
